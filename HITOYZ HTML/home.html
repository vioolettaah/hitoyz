Your Inner Child's Missing Piece

Started on July 7th, 2019, as a family-owned local figurine store, HI-Toyz grew in fame during the end of 2020, with an increasing number of local collectors visiting the store. At its second anniversary, HI-Toyz gained its breakthrough into the toy industry and started its expansion to multiple regions.

Now, HI-Toyz is a well-established figurine and toy store with over 20+ offline stores around the country and is the official reseller for major toy companies such as LEGO, Tomica, Bandai, and MATTEL.

OF THE DAY
Snow Miku: Snow Princess Version
The Hokkaido support character Snow Miku is now a 1/7th scale figure! Based on the theme "Princess of the Hokkaido Winter", illustrator KEI key visual of Snow Miku 2019 has been brought to life in outstanding detail. The numerous soft frills of her dress and pearly appearance of her intricate hairstyle are just two highlights of this magnificent figure.
