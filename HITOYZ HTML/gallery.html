We've come so far since the birth of our home. These are some past events and collaborations held by HI-Toyz!

HI-TOYZ X PRINCESS PRECURE COLLABORATION

In order to commemorate International Women's Day, HI-Toyz had a week-long exclusive collaboration with the Japanese magical girl franchise Princess PreCure from March 8th to March 15th, 2022. Many girls, teenagers and adults included, came to visit the area.

HI-TOYZ X POKéMON25 ANNIVERSARY

HI-Toyz celebrated Pokémon's 25th anniversary by holding a collaboration event that lasted for two months in 2021. During the time, customers would get special rewards for purchasing items worth more than Rp. 1,000,000.

HI-TOYZ @ COMIFURO-12

HI-Toyz came to Comic Frontier 12! While the event was mostly catered to anime enthusiasts, many also came to check out the latest TOMICA releases. The event lasted for two days, from November 24th to November 25th, 2022.

HI-TOYZ X SANRIO @ COMIC-CON

HI-Toyz was invited to have a special collaboration with Sanrio for COMIC-CON 2023. The booth was filled with not only Sanrio merchandises, but also many figure exhibitions in order to showcase Sanrio figurines sold by HI-Toyz.
